Xiao-Pan Pineapple Cake
Corporate Identity System

Xiao-Pan is widely recognized as a trusted Taiwanese gifting brand. This project reorganizes that familiarity into a more coherent identity that performs consistently across packaging, retail, and digital touchpoints — without losing its warmth. The redesign keeps Xiao-Pan's approachable and homelike atmosphere while introducing a more disciplined visual system. The goal is not to erase tradition, but to make it more legible, consistent, and contemporary.

The identity is built on three intersecting elements: seasonal color, monthly botanical symbols, and seal-script characters. Seasonal color reflects the temperature of the year, each botanical symbol represents the natural rhythm of its month, and the seal-script character acts as the structural and linguistic core of the composition.

Featured Highlights

The most important parts of the project are pulled forward here as a quick reading path — covering the four pillars of the identity system before presenting the full documents.

Applications Include

Logo System
The identity includes a primary logo, a compact smaller logo, and approved variations for different scales and formats. Clear space, minimum size, misuse rules, and background guidelines ensure consistent reproduction across all media.
Color Identity
Base and sub colors build a warm brand palette shaped by pastry tones, gift-box cues, and calm contrast. The system supports both print and digital use while maintaining a recognizable brand atmosphere.
Typeface
Chinese and English typefaces are selected for legibility and warmth. The typographic system establishes a clear hierarchy across packaging labels, promotional materials, and digital interfaces.
Supporting Graphics
Botanical motifs, pattern applications, and the twelve-month seasonal module extend the identity into time-based applications — including calendars, marketing materials, and selected packaging.

Monthly Design System

Development of twelve seasonal modules through color, botanical symbol, and seal-script character.

Key Applications

Four application groups best demonstrate the identity performing across physical, spatial, and digital contexts — from unit packaging and gift boxes to staff uniforms, storefront signage, and responsive website interfaces.

A brand identity, when built with care, becomes the bridge between a product and its culture — transforming daily gestures like gifting pineapple cakes into an expression of shared identity, seasonal rhythm, and cultural continuity. Xiao-Pan carries this in every detail, from the seal-script character to the gift box ribbon.
