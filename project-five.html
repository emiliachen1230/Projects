Design Purpose

This kettle is designed for individuals who value both luxury and convenience in their everyday lives. It offers a perfect balance of elegance and functionality, catering to those who want an easy-to-use yet stylish appliance that complements a sophisticated home. Ideal for tea lovers who appreciate high-quality, beautifully designed products, this kettle fits seamlessly into modern or elegant environments, whether in the kitchen or living room.

User Type
Usage Environment

Goal

The goal is to create a kettle that's both stylish and highly functional. It aims to make tea-making quick and easy, while looking beautiful in any setting. The kettle is designed to blend luxury design with everyday practicality.
Opportunity
This kettle brings a modern touch to traditional tea-making. Its sleek, wireless design makes it more than just a kitchen tool — it's a piece of decor that enhances any space. The opportunity lies in offering a product that combines beauty with convenience, appealing to those who value both.
Feature
Quick Boiling: Heats water fast for easy tea-making.
Elegant Design: Inspired by Georg Jensen's sleek and stylish design.
No Leaks: Designed to prevent spills and make brewing hassle-free.
Easy to Use: Simple and user-friendly for everyday tea preparation.
Style
The kettle has a smooth, polished look with flowing lines, making it both functional and beautiful. Its elegant design adds a touch of luxury to any space, turning tea-making into an enjoyable and stylish experience.
