Rondo / Door Bell Device

For three decades, the craft behind Rondo has cultivated a legacy of excellence — transforming everyday interactions into breathtaking sensory experiences. Founded on a love for music and exceptional craftsmanship, Rondo bridges people through melody and connection. At its core, Rondo crafts more than a doorbell; it forges connections between people and their shared spaces.

Rondo Door Bell
Interaction Device

Rondo is a unique doorbell that uses music to create a warm connection between people. When someone enters a room, its key-sensing technology plays beautiful melodies, making the experience of entering more special. Crafted with Arduino programming and featuring Bluetooth, the five piano keys play automatically based on sensory feedback — allowing users to create their own tunes and share emotions through music, making daily life more enjoyable.

Services Include

Function
Touch the piano keys, and Rondo automatically plays melodies. This feature lets users create their own music or send secret messages through sound. Its Bluetooth function also allows users to stream music, adding to the interactive experience and atmosphere.
Symbol
Rondo's design is inspired by brass instruments, making it easy for people to recognize and use. The device produces rich, resonant sounds that fill the room, engaging users in a unique musical experience.

Interaction Design

The interactive device uses key-sensing technology to automatically play music, blending music and technology in a new way. This creates a fresh and exciting experience for users, breaking traditional limits between the two. By combining these elements, it not only enhances how people enjoy music but also brings a new level of creativity to art and culture, making it a big step forward in connecting technology with musical expression.

Music, as a universally embraced language that pervades cultures worldwide, possesses the unique ability to transcend national and cultural barriers. It unites people from diverse backgrounds in a shared emotional experience, fostering cultural exchange and mutual understanding through the universal language of melody and rhythm.
