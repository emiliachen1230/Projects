Diving +

Diving+ is designed to connect the global diving community while highlighting Taiwan's unique marine ecosystem. By offering a platform for sharing diving experiences and marine ecology knowledge, we aim to make underwater exploration more engaging and accessible — establishing Diving+ as a leading international hub for divers worldwide.

Design Motivation

Diving+ is designed to connect the global diving community while highlighting Taiwan's unique marine ecosystem. By offering a platform for sharing diving experiences and marine ecology knowledge, we aim to make underwater exploration more engaging and accessible. Our vision is to establish Diving+ as a leading international hub for divers, fostering a deeper appreciation for marine conservation and promoting sustainable diving practices worldwide.

Goal

The goal is to make the underwater world more accessible to everyone. Diving+ bridges the gap between land and sea by offering a user-friendly platform that connects a global diving community, promotes marine conservation, and provides the tools needed for safe and sustainable diving.

Solution
Diving+ is a comprehensive platform for divers to share experiences, access detailed dive site information, connect with professional instructors, and engage in environmental sustainability efforts.
Quality
Designed for optimal user experience, the app offers intuitive navigation, dependable features, and high-quality content, catering to both novice and seasoned divers.
Function
Easy access to dive site details, a platform for sharing experiences, connections to instructors, and tools for environmental conservation — all through a user-friendly interface.

Current Survey

The current survey reveals a clear profile of Diving+ users and their habits. Scuba diving with oxygen tanks is the dominant activity at 70.2%, while the largest age group is 15 to 25 years old at 72.6%. In addition, 59.6% of respondents dive five times or less per year, suggesting that the platform mainly serves beginners and light-frequency recreational divers.

Survey Reading

User Profile

Most participants are young divers in the 15 to 25 age range, suggesting a strong opportunity to position Diving+ as an accessible entry point into diving culture.

Diving Frequency

Since most respondents dive only a few times per year, the interface should prioritize clarity, onboarding support, and quick access to essential information.

Activity Preference

Scuba diving remains the primary activity, so content around equipment, safety, certification, and dive-site discovery should remain central.

User Analysis × Journey Map

This map translates the brand strategy into user behavior, showing how Diving+ attracts attention, supports evaluation, and guides users toward app download and course participation.

Stage 01

Before Download

Initial awareness is built through Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, Spotify, and podcasts, positioning diving not only as a sport but also as a social and lifestyle-oriented experience.

Stage 02

Evaluation Stage

Users begin comparing information quality, store credibility, and learning accessibility. This stage requires a visual language that feels trustworthy, readable, and easy to navigate.

Stage 03

After Download

The journey is designed to lead users toward two main outcomes: successfully downloading and using the platform, and successfully enrolling in diving-related courses and activities.

Persona

Persona Insight

Elsa, a 32-year-old manager with extensive diving experience, represents a user driven by exploration, ocean preference, and meaningful outdoor engagement. Her profile helps define both interface tone and service priorities.

Growth

Promotion Strategy

Referrals from friends, family, and diving partners play a stronger role than advertising alone, while Facebook marketing supports visibility. This indicates that community trust is central to the platform’s growth model.

Value

Business Positioning

The service connects two key values: course instruction and tourism aspects. Diving+ therefore works as both a learning platform and a gateway into wider underwater culture and travel-oriented experiences.

CIS Overview

The corporate identity system for Diving+ is developed to connect diving culture, social interaction, and service accessibility through a clear and approachable visual language. Rather than functioning only as a brand surface, the CIS is intended to support trust, community participation, and the transition from curiosity to actual course enrollment.

01

Brand Positioning

Diving+ is positioned as an accessible yet reliable platform for young divers and beginners. The identity aims to reduce the distance between diving as a professional field and diving as a social, cultural, and lifestyle experience.

02

Visual Language

The visual system combines marine-inspired blue tones, simplified interface structures, and soft geometric shapes to communicate clarity, trust, exploration, and environmental awareness across both digital and promotional applications.
