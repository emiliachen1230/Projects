Foody Foody / Food Order

Foody Foody is an integrated food-waste reduction platform developed near National Taiwan Normal University. It combines a consumer-facing mobile app, a LINE-based ordering system, and a restaurant backend to connect surplus food with people who need it — reducing waste, tracking carbon emissions, and rewarding eco-friendly behavior along the way.

Motivation

In 2021, the UN Food and Agriculture Organization highlighted a critical disparity: while 828 million people globally suffered from hunger, nearly one-third of all produced food was wasted. This alarming gap inspired us to seek solutions that reduce food waste and align with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) for responsible consumption and production.

Design Purpose

Address the issue of food waste from both consumers and businesses — reduce waste volume, save energy and reduce carbon emissions, mitigate global warming and climate change, and create a positive cycle.

Goal

The goal is to reduce food waste and redirect surplus food to those in need through innovative and sustainable methods. By developing this platform, we aim to support the fair distribution of food resources and actively contribute to global efforts against hunger, while also caring for the environment.

Solution
The app links users to local stores with surplus food. It tracks carbon emissions and rewards eco-friendly actions, encouraging users to make sustainable choices.
Quality
The LINE ordering system and app are simple and user-friendly. Key features include order history with portion details, ensuring reliability and ease of use.
Function
Key functions include a map of stores with surplus food, carbon emission tracking, and rewards for eco-friendly actions. Users can also view past orders and portions.
