Author

About
Me

Emilia Chen works across interaction design, systems thinking, visual identity, and interdisciplinary research. Her practice moves between engineering precision and aesthetic construction, treating interfaces, products, and images as cultural forms rather than neutral tools.

She is interested in how technology reorganizes perception, memory, and communication, and develops works that connect software, material form, and visual language. The approach is both technical and reflective, grounded in making, analysis, and iterative experimentation.

Focus

Interaction design, new media, systems, visual culture, artistic research.

Method

Prototyping, UI/UX, computational thinking, branding, front-end implementation.

Perspective

Cross-disciplinary work connecting technology, design, aesthetics, and critical inquiry.
